Student guide

Preparation advice for the exchange period before departure.

Suggested timeline

  • 2–3 months beforeChoose courses, confirm destination deadlines, start your Learning Agreement.
  • 6–8 weeks beforeArrange accommodation, insurance and travel documents.
  • 2–4 weeks beforeCheck transport, upload missing files and review official instructions.
  • Final weekVerify departure details, emergency contacts and arrival information.

Useful reminders

  • Make sure your insurance covers the entire mobility period.
  • Keep signed versions of your documents in one folder.
  • Confirm accommodation address before booking travel.
  • Check your host university orientation dates.
