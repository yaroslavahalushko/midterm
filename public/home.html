For VDU exchange students

Keep deadlines, documents and semester planning in one place.

Erasmus Semester Planner is a student-focused prototype that makes Erasmus preparation easier to understand. Instead of keeping deadlines in many tabs and notes, the planner shows the most important information in one interface.

After login, you can move between the planner dashboard, your profile page and the student guide using the header icons.

What you can do in the planner

The prototype follows the main Erasmus preparation steps from account entry to final document checks.

🧾

Prepare your semester

View your destination, semester status, application countdown and current priorities on the dashboard.

✅

Manage tasks dynamically

Add custom tasks, mark them complete, filter results and keep a clear overview of pending steps.

📄

Track important files

Preview document names like your Learning Agreement, insurance confirmation and travel documents.

Recommended preparation flow

1

Login

Enter the website through the login screen and open the planner pages.

2

Complete profile

Save your destination, semester and personal notes on the dedicated profile page.

3

Plan tasks

Add application, agreement, housing and travel preparation tasks with their deadline.

4

Check documents

Keep document names and required preparation steps visible before departure.

Why students need this

  • Exchange preparation often comes from many emails, websites and notes.
  • A single dashboard reduces confusion and missed deadlines.
  • Students can understand what still needs to be done before mobility starts.
  • The layout stays usable on both laptop and mobile screens.
